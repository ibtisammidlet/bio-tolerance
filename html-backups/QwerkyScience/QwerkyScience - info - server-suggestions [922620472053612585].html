qwerkyscience 12/27/2021 7:18 PM
This sounds interesting. I have mixed feelings about supplementation. Supplements can be very interesting in their effects but I’m more suspicious about their ability to solve practical problems. Not in all cases though. That’s a topic I could address. I think many issues we face are ones that could be better confronted in relation to our thinking patterns and how these cause us to suffer or experience motivational issues or other problems. Supplements and drugs can change our feelings but much less often our beliefs. Drugs like psychedelics might sort of bend our beliefs though it seems fairly difficult to practically implement belief change. Often times our feelings and motivational problems stem from beliefs and their effects on our behavior and feelings.
7:20 PM
As an example, I struggle to find motivation to learn teleportation, despite that I strongly desire such a fantastic ability. It isn’t my lack of desire that causes my anhedonia for learning teleportation. It is my belief that teleportation isn’t achievable that causes my anhedonia. I could use cannabis and amphetamines to raise my motivation for achieving teleportation, though of course this enters territory like delusion
7:21 PM
I actually believe a lot of the nootropics community is trying to address problems with beliefs by modifying them or the feelings we have because of them by using drugs.
7:23 PM
For example, low confidence in our ability to reach a goal like succeeding in class or socially could result in an apathy like I have for teleportation. Succeeding in these ways could appear as difficult as achieving teleportation. The belief that succession is unattainable results in apathy. One could solve such a problem via talking and exploring the problem. Possibly better than using drugs that produce a sense that we should take more risks with unbelievable goals.

I'd have made most of the channels except the main 4 hidden to all but a few users like mods and admins and people with noteworthy technical knowledge. And unhide them for the rest of the community when activity ramped back up. Ofc there are many ways to run a server, this was just what I found to work best for me. (edited)
