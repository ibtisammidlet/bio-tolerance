Pely 5/5/2023 8:34 PM
Exact ratio depends on the plants, for Ipomoea batatas I'd say Coco coir up to 20% sand (and rocks at the bottom of the bed) 15% perlite 5% charcoal (grind it up as much as possible) Fertilizer of choice (if worm castings use at least 20%) The more sand and perlite you add, the more frequently you'll have to irrigate. Some plants need a very well drained soil, some need more nutrients (every time you irrigate and it drains, you lose some nutrients). Batat needs a lot of nutrients esp phosphorus and potassium, but also nitrogen in the first few months.
